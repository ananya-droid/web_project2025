Know Your Rights

Be informed about your consumer rights and learn how to take action against fraud, scams, and unfair practices.

Consumer Rights

Right to Safety

Consumers have the right to be protected from hazardous goods and services that can cause harm.

Right to Information

Consumers must receive accurate details about products, including ingredients, price, and risks.

Right to Choose

Consumers have the freedom to select from a variety of quality goods and services at fair prices.

Right to Be Heard

Consumers can voice their grievances and expect their complaints to be addressed fairly.

Right to Redress

Consumers are entitled to compensation or replacement for defective products and services.

Right to Consumer Education

Consumers have the right to acquire knowledge and skills to make informed choices.

Common Consumer Complaints & Solutions

Defective Products

Solution: Contact the seller for a refund/replacement. If denied, file a complaint with consumer forums.

False Advertising

Solution: Report misleading ads to the advertising standards council or regulatory authorities.

Online Fraud

Solution: Use secure payment methods, verify sellers, and report fraud to cybercrime authorities.

Poor Customer Service

Solution: Escalate the issue to higher management, post reviews, or seek legal support.

Billing Errors

Solution: Contact customer support for corrections. If unresolved, seek help from a consumer agency.
